Paperboard Selection

Optimize your packaging with the best paperboard for your products, sourced both domestically and internationally. 

  • Technical Assessment
  • Global Paperboard Expertise
  • Virgin and Recyled Paperboard Alternatives
  • FSC Certified

Sheeting & Slitting

We sheet in-house, allowing Century Box to maximize the economy of roll utilization. 

Job-specific paperboard sheets for faster turn-around times.

  • Precision Sheeting to get you to Press more Efficiently
  • Oversized Roll Trimming/Slitting
  • Automatic Sheet Cleaning
  • Quality Checks for Squareness and Caliper

Printing: Conventional & UV

We utilize both offset and flexographic technology in narrow web, mid-sized sheets and large format sheets to meet the diversity of your product needs.

Your Packaging will Shine when Printed on our Offset and Flexographic Presses.

  • Conventional and Extended Gamut
  • UV Inks and Coatings
  • Closed Loop Color Control Systems

Die Cutting

Our Die Cutting Operators convert your product from a printed sheet into individual die cut blanks. 

Lazer cut die boards, in-house die knifing, and blanker construction deliver optimum in-process capability.

  • Emboss and Deboss
  • Sheet Register Systems
  • Opening Features and Apertures

Finishing​

We have the capability to window, glue, assemble and pack inline on a variety of configurations of speciality machines, which are carton design specific.

Multiple Options to Get Your Cartons Ready for Deployment in Your Packaging Workflow.

  • Gluing: All Folding Carton Styles
  • In-Line Windowing
  • Automatic Case Packing and Robotic Palletization
  • Gluing and Assembly
  • Speciality Applications (Bundling Tipping & Labeling)
